Han Roro

Han Roro

Han Roro is a South Korean singer-songwriter who debuted on March 14, 2022.

Hanroro's music is rooted in modern rock, characterized by poetic and lyrical writing that deeply captures the emotions and experiences of youth. Her poignant vocals and band sound, centered around electric guitar, effectively express the anxieties and emptiness of youth. At times, she incorporates powerful and fast-paced sounds to bring vibrancy to her songs.

Her literary lyrics are a crucial element of her music. She delicately portrays various emotions experienced by young people, such as emptiness, alienation, and wistfulness, offering comfort and resonance to many listeners.
